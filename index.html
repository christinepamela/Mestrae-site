Where we are

Rebuilding.
Refunding.
Returning.

Between 2019 and 2021, Mestrae was hit by an unpaid grant, the pandemic, and severe flooding — each one compounding the last. We lost our manufacturing facility, our inventory, and years of work. Rebuilding has been slow and personal. But we are still here, and we are doing right by everyone who believed in us. All affected customers will be refunded by June 2026. The shop reopens November 1. Step by step.
