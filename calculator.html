Find your ideal heel height.

Six questions about how you live and move. We will match you to the heel height that makes the most sense for your day — and tell you why.

Question 1 of 6
What are you mainly wearing heels for?
Think about the occasions that come up most often for you.
Question 2 of 6
On a typical day in heels, how long do you usually wear them?
This is about your usual experience, not your ideal. Be honest with yourself.
Question 3 of 6
How would you describe your experience with heels?
There is no wrong answer. Confidence in heels is learned, not inherited.
Question 4 of 6
What surfaces do you usually walk on in heels?
This affects stability more than most people realise.
Question 5 of 6
When you wear heels, what matters most to you?
Pick the one that feels most true, even if more than one applies.
Question 6 of 6
Do you have any specific foot or body concerns that affect how you wear shoes?
This helps us give you the most honest recommendation.

A note on medical conditions
Mestrae is not a medical or orthopaedic shoe. If you have bunions, plantar fasciitis, back pain, or a structural foot condition, we would recommend consulting a specialist who can assess your specific needs. We know what those conditions require — and we are not there yet. We will let you know when we are.
