Tools are how we think
about customer care.

Most shoe brands expect you to figure it out yourself. Mestrae was built by an engineer, and engineers believe that the right information at the right moment makes everything easier. These tools exist because buying the wrong size, the wrong heel height, or a shoe you do not know how to care for is a problem we can prevent before it happens.

  • Every tool is free, no account required to use it
  • No recommendations are tied to upselling a more expensive product
  • The comfort timer exists because we believe heels should not hurt
  • The size guide was written for our specific shoes, not copied from a generic chart
  • If a tool does not answer your question, email us directly
